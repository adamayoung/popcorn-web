Privacy first

What's yours stays yours

Popcorn is designed to be enjoyable without asking you to hand over your identity. There's no sign-up, and the personal touches happen on your device or in your own iCloud.

  • ✓ No account, name or email required to use the app.
  • ✓ Your watchlist syncs through your private iCloud — the developer can't see it.
  • ✓ The movie companion runs on-device with Apple Intelligence; your chats aren't sent to the developer.
  • ✓ No advertising, and your data is never sold.
